Common Questions

    • How do I retrieve my saved quote later?

      To retrieve you saved quote we will need your last name, date of birth, zip code and your quote number or email address. If you are unable to retrieve your quote, please contact your Farmers agent for assistance.

    • What are some of the factors that determine my auto insurance cost?

      1. Your lowest rates on car insurance depends on what you drive.

      2. It depends on the deductible: the higher the deductible, the lower the premium - but the more you'll pay out of pocket in case of a claim. The lower the deductible, the higher the premium - but the less you'll pay if you have a covered claim.

      3. It depends on the limit: the lower the limit, the lower the premium - but the less protection your insurance policy will provide to pay for damages you cause in an accident. The higher the limit, the higher the premium - but the more protection your insurance policy will provide to pay for damages you cause in an accident.
